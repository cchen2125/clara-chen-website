Predicting Star Ratings of Yelp Reviews*

Developing a Sentiment Analysis Language Model

2023

Can we use the text of Yelp user reviews to predict the star ratings for reviews of businesses in Philadelphia? To answer this question, we built NLP models (LSTM, BERT, etc.) that takes a Yelp review and predicts the star rating. Then, to see whether star ratings accurately match sentiment analysis, we compare both the true star rating labels and our predicted star ratings to the sentiment analysis results of a pre-trained model from Hugging Face.

Tools and Languages: Python, Jupyter Notebook

Segregated Success

An Analysis of Upward Mobility in Hartford, CT

2022

Despite being known as the “insurance capital of the world,” Hartford suffers a growing poverty rate, ranking as one of the poorest cities in the U.S. Given this dichotomy, how likely are children in Hartford to experience upward economic mobility and what factors influence their chances? I investigate these question by analyzing data from Opportunity Insights.
